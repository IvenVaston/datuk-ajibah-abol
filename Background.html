Background

Datuk Ajibah Abol Children's Home, Sri Aman was completed in April 1993 on a land area of ​​8.64 acres. It was named as such to honor the former Minister of General Welfare, YB Datuk Adibah Abol. The official opening was made on September 29, 1993 by YABhg. Datuk Patinggi Hajah Rosmiati, D.P, S.P.M.B. (Brunei), wife of His Excellency the President of Sarawak. The Children's Home located at KM 5, Jalan Sabu, Sri Aman has an Administration Block, a Multipurpose Hall, a kitchen, a prayer room, an activity room, a classroom, a Treatment Room Block, a block for Vocational Training, 4 Twin male and female Dormitory Block with Junior General Assistant Block. A Lower General Auxiliary Block has been reserved for the Baby Room. This house can accommodate as many as 100 children. While each Hostel Block can accommodate a total of 25 children.

Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol, located in the picturesque town of Sri Aman, is a haven for orphaned and abandoned children. Established with the vision of providing a loving and supportive home environment, this orphanage is named in honor of Datuk Ajibah Abol, a respected community leader known for her dedication to the welfare of children. The institution is nestled amidst lush greenery, surrounded by trees and vibrant gardens that create a serene atmosphere conducive to the holistic development of the children. The physical infrastructure of the home is designed to ensure comfort, safety, and a sense of belonging for every resident. The orphanage follows a comprehensive care approach, focusing not only on meeting the basic needs of the children but also on their education, health, and emotional well-being. Trained and compassionate caregivers, including teachers, nurses, and support staff, work together to create a nurturing environment that fosters growth and positive development. Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol is committed to providing quality education to its residents, partnering with local schools and educational institutions to ensure that every child receives an education tailored to their needs and abilities. The institution actively engages with the local community, organizing outreach programs and events that promote awareness and support for the well-being of orphaned children. Through collaborations with volunteers and donors, Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol strives to create a network of care that extends beyond its walls. In essence, Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol in Sri Aman stands as a beacon of hope and compassion, dedicated to offering orphaned children a loving home and a promising future.
