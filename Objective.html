RUMAH KANAK KANAK DATUK AJIBAH ABOL,SRI AMAN

Objective

The objective of this Home is to provide care and protection to children and promote healthy growth and development (Physical, Mental, Emotional) in a peaceful and harmonious environment.

Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol in Sri Aman aims to provide a nurturing and supportive environment for children who have been orphaned, abandoned, or are facing challenging circumstances. The primary objectives include ensuring the physical, emotional, and psychological well-being of the children under their care. This involves providing a safe and secure living space, nutritious meals, and access to proper healthcare. Additionally, the home likely focuses on the educational development of the children, aiming to provide them with quality education and learning opportunities. The objective is to empower these children with the necessary skills and knowledge to lead independent and fulfilling lives in the future. Furthermore, Rumah Kanak-Kanak Datuk Ajibah Abol may emphasize the importance of cultivating social skills and a sense of community among the children. Creating a supportive and inclusive environment helps the children build positive relationships with each other and with the caregivers. The home may also strive to reintegrate children with their extended families or facilitate their adoption when suitable. Ultimately, the overarching goal is to give these children a chance at a brighter future by providing them with the care, education, and emotional support they need during their formative years.
